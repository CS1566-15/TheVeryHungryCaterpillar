--------------------------------------------------------------------------
The caterpillar will have an ability to pick up fruits and eat them later.
There will be a small circular meter display time until effect ends.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. STRAWBERRY: Add an extra jump
	-> No. of times the player can jump is limited.
2. ORANGE: Increase jump distance for a limited time
	-> Platforms that can only be reached with a long jump.
3. BLUEBERRY: Increase move speed for limited time
	-> Hot areas of ground (pavement) can tread for limited time.
4. CHOCOLATE: Point the player toward nearby foods
	-> Food can be hard to locate
5. GRAPE: Double growth rate for a limited time
	-> Maximize growth when lots of food nearby
6. CAKE: Allow the player to jump and smash through walls 1 time.
	-> There are walls in the level that must be smashed through
7. POISON FRUIT: Shrinks the player's size.
	-> Acts as a level hazard
8. SEED: No effect, basic, introductory food
	-> Grow the caterpillar's size
